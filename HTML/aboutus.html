About Watches Center

The Watches center includes many distinctive watches from international brands. Watches Center is a leading website in the field of selling watches in Jordan and includes a group of watches with international brands. Watches Center Group also sells smartwatches to various international companies. We care about the finest details and high quality of our products.

With these major brands in the watch market, watches Center is in a position to provide local consumers with a wide range of brands at various prices.

These different sectors are complemented by fully equipped and approved after-sales service centers. A management team of more than 30 employees, based in the prestigious Al Rabieh district, oversees the group's activities. The department includes Brand Management Department, Retail Department, Marketing Department, Wholesale Department, Corporate Sales Department, Finance Department, Human Resources Department, and Information Technology Department. To complement its retail business, Watches Center Group also has under its control Al Rabieh Building, which houses office and retail space.
